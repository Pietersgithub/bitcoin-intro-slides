## What is a "Currency" - Medium of exchange. - Measure of value.
(providing a unit of value) - Store of value. Example: gold, cash bills, bank accounts.

What is Bitcoin?

First decentralized digital currency.
bitcoin.org
A purely peer-to-peer version of electronic cash would allow online payments to be sent directly from one party to another without going through a financial institution.
Satoshi Nakamoto - the bitcoin whitepaper
## What's behind Bitcoin - Game Theory - Cryptography - Distributed Networking, Consensus - Economic Theory
## What's new? The first time in history we have a digital asset that can be transferred but cannot be duplicated. - Solves the problem of "double spending". - Decentralized - Trustless: no financial institutions, governments... - Censorship resistant. - Resilient and Secure. - Permissionless: low cost bank for everyone. - Transnational, borderless.
## What is the Blockchain? - Distributed Database. - Records are "Blocks" of transactions, chained together with cryptographic hash. - Blocks are: - Tamper-proof (verifiable) - Immutable (permanent) Blockchain is a distributed Ledger containing the full history of all bitcoins since the genesis block (3rd Jan 2009).
